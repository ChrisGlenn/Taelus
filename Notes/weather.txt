*** WEATHER ***
The weather in Taelus...
If the weather has an * it will cause player to move slower

sunny/windy days
SUN - sunny day
OVERCAST - overcast day
WIND - windy day
WIND_STORM -*- really windy day

rainy days
LIGHT_RAIN - light sprinkling
RAIN - basic rain
HEAVY_RAIN -*- large raindrops
RAIN_STORM -*- windy 'basic' rain

snowy days
LIGHT_SNOW - light snow (white dots)
SNOW - basic snow (larger white dots)
HEAVY_SNOW -*- larger white dots w/ snowflakes
SNOW_STORM -*- larger white dots w/ wind and snowflakes
